Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the time converter?

Our time converter is 100% accurate for standard time units. It uses precise conversion factors: 1 minute = 60 seconds, 1 hour = 3600 seconds, 1 day = 86400 seconds, etc. For months and years, we use average values (30.44 days per month, 365.25 days per year) to account for variations.

Can I convert between very large time values?

Yes! Our converter can handle extremely large values (up to 1e+308) and extremely small values (down to 1e-308). You can convert nanoseconds to years, millennia to seconds, or any combination you need.

How do you handle leap years?

For standard conversions, we use the average year length of 365.25 days (accounting for leap years). For precise astronomical or calendar calculations, you may need specialized tools, but for everyday use, our conversions are perfectly accurate.

What's the smallest time unit you support?

We support nanoseconds (1 billionth of a second) as the smallest unit. You can convert from nanoseconds all the way up to millennia and beyond. The converter maintains precision up to 15 decimal places for most calculations.

Is my conversion data stored?

No! All conversions happen locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or collect any of your data. Your privacy is completely protected, and you can use the tool offline once loaded.
