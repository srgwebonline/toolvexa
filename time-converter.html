Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the Time Converter?

Our Time Converter is highly accurate, using standardized conversion factors. For months, we use 30 days as a standard month (not calendar months) and 365 days for a standard year (not accounting for leap years). This provides consistent results suitable for most calculations.

Can I convert between very large time units?

Yes! You can convert between any time units from nanoseconds to years. The tool handles very large and very small values efficiently. For extremely large values (like centuries), we recommend converting to years first for optimal precision.

What time units are supported?

We support: nanoseconds, microseconds, milliseconds, seconds, minutes, hours, days, weeks, months (30-day standard), and years (365-day standard). You can convert between any of these units instantly.

How does the month conversion work?

We use a standard 30-day month for consistent calculations. This is a common practice in time conversion tools. If you need calendar month accuracy (28-31 days), we recommend using days as your base unit instead.

Can I use decimal values?

Absolutely! You can use decimal values like 1.5 hours, 0.25 days, or 3.75 weeks. The converter handles decimal inputs precisely, giving you accurate decimal results for your conversions.

Is there a limit to how many conversions I can do?

No limits at all! Our Time Converter is 100% free with unlimited usage. You can convert as many values as you need, as often as you need. All calculations happen locally in your browser, so there are no server limitations.
