Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about document conversion

Is this document converter really free to use?

Yes, our document converter is completely free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. You can convert unlimited documents without any limitations.

What document formats are supported?

We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, PPT, PPTX, TXT, and RTF formats. You can convert between any of these formats with our tool.

Is there a file size limit for documents?

You can convert documents up to 50MB in size. For larger files, we recommend compressing them first or splitting them into smaller parts.

How long are my files stored on your servers?

Your files are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after conversion. We don't store any documents permanently.

Does the converter preserve formatting?

Yes, our converter preserves formatting, fonts, images, and layout to the best possible extent during conversion between formats.

Can I convert multiple documents at once?

Currently, we support single document conversion. For batch conversion, you can use our document multiple times or check out our batch processing tools.
