Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Document Converter really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our Document Converter is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can convert as many documents as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone with their document processing needs.

Are my documents safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All document processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your documents never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your files.

What document formats do you support?

We support all popular document formats including PDF, DOC, DOCX (Word), TXT (Plain Text), RTF (Rich Text Format), ODT (OpenDocument), HTML, and more. You can convert between any of these formats with our tool.

Will my formatting be preserved during conversion?

Our conversion algorithm is designed to preserve as much formatting as possible. For conversions between similar formats (like DOC to DOCX), formatting is preserved almost perfectly. For conversions between different format types (like PDF to TXT), basic structure is preserved but some complex formatting may be simplified.

Can I convert multiple documents at once?

Yes! You can upload and convert up to 10 documents simultaneously. Our batch processing feature saves you time when working with multiple documents. Each document is processed individually with your selected settings, and you can download them all together or individually.

How accurate is the conversion?

Our conversion is highly accurate for text and basic formatting. For complex documents with advanced formatting, tables, images, and special elements, the accuracy may vary. We recommend checking the converted document before final use, especially for important documents.
