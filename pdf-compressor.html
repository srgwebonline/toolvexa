Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Compressor really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our PDF Compressor is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can compress as many PDFs as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone manage their documents efficiently.

Are my PDFs safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your documents.

How much can I compress my PDFs?

Compression depends on the original PDF content. Typically, you can reduce file size by 50-90% without noticeable quality loss. PDFs with high-resolution images compress the most. Text-only PDFs have less compression potential. Use the different compression levels to find the right balance for your needs.

Will compression affect PDF quality?

Our compression algorithm is designed to reduce file size while maintaining visual quality. At "Print Quality" setting, the compression is nearly lossless. For web or email use, we apply more aggressive compression that may slightly reduce image quality but keeps text crisp and clear. We recommend previewing the compressed file before finalizing.

Can I compress multiple PDFs at once?

Yes! You can upload and compress up to 5 PDFs simultaneously. Our batch processing feature saves you time when working with multiple documents. Each PDF is processed individually with your selected settings, and you can download them all together or individually.

What types of PDFs compress best?

PDFs with high-resolution images (scanned documents, photos, graphics) compress the most - often 70-90% reduction. Text-heavy documents with few images compress moderately (30-50%). Already optimized PDFs may only compress 10-20%. The "Maximum Compression" preset works best for image-heavy PDFs.
