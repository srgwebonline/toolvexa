Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert kilograms to pounds?

To convert kilograms to pounds, multiply the kilogram value by 2.20462. For example, 1 kg = 2.20462 lbs. Our converter does this automatically - just enter the value in kilograms and select pounds as the target unit.

What is the difference between metric and imperial weight units?

Metric units (kilograms, grams, milligrams) are based on the decimal system and used internationally. Imperial units (pounds, ounces, stones) are primarily used in the United States and United Kingdom. 1 kilogram equals 2.20462 pounds, and 1 pound equals 0.453592 kilograms.

How accurate is the weight converter?

Our weight converter uses precise conversion factors based on international standards. For example, 1 pound is exactly 0.45359237 kilograms. The tool calculates with high precision and displays results with appropriate decimal places for each unit.

What are stones used for in weight measurement?

Stones are an imperial unit of weight primarily used in the UK and Ireland for body weight. One stone equals 14 pounds or approximately 6.35029 kilograms. While not commonly used in scientific contexts, it's still widely used for personal weight measurements in those regions.

Can I convert carats with this tool?

Yes, our converter includes carats as a unit option. Carats are used for measuring gemstones and pearls. One metric carat equals 200 milligrams or 0.2 grams. The converter can convert carats to any other weight unit in the list.

Is my data secure when using this converter?

Absolutely. All conversions happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. No data is sent to any server, and we don't track or store any of your conversion inputs or results. Your privacy is completely protected.
