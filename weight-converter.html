Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the weight converter?

Our weight converter uses precise conversion factors based on international standards. For example, 1 kilogram equals exactly 2.20462262185 pounds. All calculations are performed with high precision to ensure accuracy.

What's the difference between avoirdupois and troy ounces?

Our converter uses avoirdupois ounces (28.3495 grams), which are used for most everyday measurements. Troy ounces (31.1035 grams) are used primarily for precious metals. Make sure you're using the correct system for your needs.

How do I convert kilograms to pounds in my head?

A quick approximation: multiply kilograms by 2.2 to get pounds. For example, 5 kg × 2.2 = 11 lbs. For more precision, multiply by 2.20462. Our tool gives you exact conversions instantly.

Why are stones used as a weight measurement?

Stones are a traditional British unit where 1 stone equals 14 pounds. They're still commonly used in the UK and Ireland for body weight. For example, "11 stone" means 11 × 14 = 154 pounds.

Can I convert between metric and imperial units?

Yes! Our converter handles both metric (kg, g, mg) and imperial (lb, oz, st) units. You can convert between any combination of these units instantly.
