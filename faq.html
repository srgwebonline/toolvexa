Common Questions & Answers

Are Toolsedito tools really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! All Toolsedito tools are 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone improve their digital content without any barriers. There are no premium versions, subscriptions, or paywalls.

Do I need to create an account to use the tools?

No, you don't need to create an account or register to use any of our tools. All tools work immediately without any sign-up process. Just visit the tool page and start using it right away.

What file formats do your tools support?

Our image tools support all popular formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. Different tools may have specific format support, but we always list supported formats on each tool's page.

Are there any file size limits?

Most tools have generous limits (typically 10-20MB per file) to handle most common use cases. Since all processing happens in your browser, the actual limit depends on your device's memory. We recommend compressing very large files before processing.

Can I use your tools on mobile devices?

Yes! All our tools are fully responsive and work perfectly on mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adjusts automatically to your screen size for the best experience.

Is my data safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your files.

Do you store my files or personal information?

No, we don't store any files or personal information. Since all processing happens in your browser, your files never reach our servers. We don't collect personal data, and we don't use cookies for tracking.

How do you make money if everything is free?

We believe in providing free, valuable tools to everyone. Our primary goal is to help people with their digital tasks. We may display non-intrusive ads to support development costs, but we never compromise on user experience or privacy.

Are there any watermarks on processed files?

No, we never add watermarks to your files. All processed files are delivered exactly as you created them, with no branding, logos, or watermarks added.

Why does the tool say "processing locally"?

"Processing locally" means all the work happens on your device using your computer's resources. This is what ensures your privacy - your files stay with you and don't travel over the internet to our servers.

What browsers are supported?

Our tools work on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best performance and security.

Can I use your tools offline?

Most tools require an initial internet connection to load the website, but once loaded, many functions can work offline since processing happens locally. However, some features may require internet connectivity.

How accurate are your image tools?

Our image tools use advanced algorithms to ensure high accuracy. However, results may vary depending on the original file quality and complexity. We always recommend previewing results before downloading.

Can I batch process multiple files?

Yes! Most of our tools support batch processing. You can typically upload and process multiple files simultaneously, saving you time when working with multiple images or documents.

What happens if I close the browser during processing?

Since all processing happens locally, if you close the browser, your work will be lost. We recommend waiting for processing to complete and downloading your files before closing the browser tab.

Are there any usage limits or restrictions?

No, there are no usage limits. You can use our tools as much as you need, whenever you need them. We don't impose daily, weekly, or monthly limits on tool usage.

How do I report a bug or suggest a feature?

We welcome feedback! You can use the contact form on our website or email us directly. We review all suggestions and work continuously to improve our tools based on user feedback.

Can I use your tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, you can use our tools for both personal and commercial projects. There are no restrictions on how you use the processed files.

Do you comply with data protection regulations?

Yes, we're fully compliant with major data protection regulations including GDPR. Since we don't collect or store personal data, we inherently protect user privacy by design.
