Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the age calculator?

Our age calculator is 100% accurate. It calculates age based on the exact number of days between two dates, taking into account leap years and varying month lengths. The calculation follows the Gregorian calendar system used worldwide.

Can I calculate age difference between two people?

Yes! Simply enter the birth date of the first person as "Birth Date" and the birth date of the second person as "Age at the Date of". The calculator will show the exact age difference between them.

How do you handle leap years?

Our calculator automatically accounts for leap years. February 29th is included in calculations, and leap years are identified using the standard rule: years divisible by 4, except for years divisible by 100 unless also divisible by 400.

What is the maximum age I can calculate?

There's no maximum limit! You can calculate ages from 0 to any number. The calculator supports dates from year 100 to year 9999, covering all practical age calculation needs.

Is my birth date information stored?

No! All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or collect any of your personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.
