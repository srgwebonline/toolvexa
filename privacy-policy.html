Privacy Policy for Toolvexa

Last Updated: May 2026

At Toolvexa, the privacy of our visitors is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains what types of information are collected and how we use it when you use our free online tools.

TL;DR: We respect your privacy. We don't collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it. We use cookies to improve your experience, and most file processing happens locally in your browser.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it (such as through email or a contact form).

Automatically collected information may include:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time and date of your visit
  • Time spent on each page
  • Referring website (if applicable)

File Processing Information

Most Toolvexa tools process your files locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit your files to our servers unless explicitly stated for a specific tool. For tools that require server processing:

  • Files are temporarily stored for processing only
  • Files are automatically deleted after processing is complete
  • We do not access or view your files' content
  • No permanent storage of processed files

2. Cookies and Web Beacons

Toolvexa may use cookies to store visitors' preferences and improve user experience. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your computer's hard drive through your web browser (if you allow) that enables the site's systems to recognize your browser and capture and remember certain information.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Performance Cookies: Collect information about how you use our tools to help us improve

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. For more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, please consult your browser's official documentation.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our website and tools
  • To improve, personalize, and expand our services
  • To understand and analyze how you use our website
  • To develop new products, services, features, and functionality
  • To communicate with you for customer service, updates, and other website-related information
  • To prevent fraud and ensure security

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, or other functionalities. These third parties have their own privacy policies addressing how they use such information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics collects information such as how often users visit the site, what pages they visit, and what other sites they used prior to coming to our site.

Google AdSense

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Opting Out:

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication procedures
  • Secure data storage practices

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Privacy Policies

Toolvexa's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. We advise you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt out of certain options.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. To know more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, it can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

7. Children's Information

Toolvexa does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13. If you think that your child provided this kind of information on our website, we strongly encourage you to contact us immediately and we will do our best efforts to promptly remove such information from our records.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing your data
  • Objection: Object to our processing of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in Toolvexa. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.

10. Consent
