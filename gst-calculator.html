Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST and how is it calculated?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption. It's calculated as a percentage of the product or service price. The formula is: GST Amount = Original Price Ã— (GST Rate / 100). The total price including GST is: Total = Original Price + GST Amount.

Which countries' GST rates are supported?

Our GST Calculator supports multiple countries including India, Australia, Canada, Singapore, Malaysia, and New Zealand. Each country has its standard GST rates, but you can also input custom rates for specific calculations.
