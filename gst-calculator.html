Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST and why is it important?

GST (Goods and Services Tax) is a comprehensive indirect tax levied on the supply of goods and services in India. It has replaced multiple cascading taxes levied by the central and state governments. GST is important because it eliminates tax on tax, making goods and services cheaper while increasing compliance and revenue collection.

How do I add GST to an amount?

To add GST to an amount, use the "Add GST" option in the calculator. Enter the base amount, select the appropriate GST rate (5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%), and the calculator will automatically compute the GST amount and total amount including GST.

How do I remove GST from an amount?

To remove GST from an amount, use the "Remove GST" option. Enter the total amount (including GST), select the GST rate that was applied, and the calculator will separate the GST amount from the base amount.

What are the different GST rates in India?

The main GST rates in India are: 0% (essential items), 5% (common use items), 12% (standard rate), 18% (general rate), and 28% (luxury items). Some special rates like 0.25%, 1%, and 3% apply to specific items like precious stones and diamonds.
