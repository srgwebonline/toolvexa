How does the AI image generator work?

Our tool uses advanced AI models that have been trained on millions of images and text descriptions. When you enter a prompt, the AI analyzes the text and generates a completely new image that matches your description.

Can I use the generated images commercially?

Yes, all images generated with our tool are free to use for both personal and commercial purposes. You can use them in your projects, websites, social media, or products without attribution.

What are the best practices for writing prompts?

Be specific about subjects, styles, colors, and composition. Include details like "photorealistic", "oil painting", "cyberpunk style", or "4K resolution". Use adjectives to describe mood and atmosphere. The more detailed your prompt, the better the results.
