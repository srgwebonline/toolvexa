Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a discount percentage?

To calculate a discount percentage, subtract the sale price from the original price, divide that amount by the original price, and then multiply by 100. Our calculator does this automatically - just enter the original price and the discount percentage you want to apply.

Can I calculate reverse discounts (find original price from sale price)?

Yes! If you know the sale price and the discount percentage, you can calculate the original price. The formula is: Original Price = Sale Price รท (1 - Discount Percentage/100). Simply enter the sale price as the "original price" and use a negative discount percentage (not yet implemented in this version).

Does the calculator work with tax included?

Our calculator shows the discount amount before tax. If you need to include tax, calculate the discount first, then add your local sales tax to the final sale price. We're working on adding a tax-inclusive option in a future update.

Can I use the calculator for bulk or multiple items?

Absolutely! For multiple identical items, calculate the discount for one item and multiply by the quantity. For items with different prices, you'll need to calculate each separately. Business users often use our calculator for pricing entire product lines with consistent discount rates.

Is the calculator accurate for all currencies?

Yes, the calculator works with any currency. The calculations are based on percentages, which work the same regardless of currency. Just enter your amounts in your local currency - the tool doesn't convert between currencies but will calculate discounts accurately for any numeric value.

Can I calculate stacked discounts (multiple discounts)?

For stacked discounts, calculate the first discount, then use that result as the new "original price" for the second discount. Our current version handles single discounts, but we're planning to add a multiple discount calculator in the future for complex discount scenarios.
