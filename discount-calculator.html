About Discount Calculation

How Discounts Work

Discounts reduce the original price by a certain percentage. A 20% discount on a $100 item means you pay $80, saving $20.

Shopping Tips

Compare discounts carefully. A "50% off then additional 20% off" is not the same as "70% off" - it's actually 60% off the original price.

Business Applications

Businesses use discounts to attract customers, clear inventory, and boost sales during seasonal promotions and special events.

Common Mistakes

Don't confuse percentage off with percentage of. Also, remember that taxes are usually calculated on the sale price, not the original price.
