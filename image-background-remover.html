Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Is the Background Remover really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our Background Remover is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can remove backgrounds from as many images as you need without any restrictions.

Are my images safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All image processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support all popular image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF. The processed images are downloaded as PNG files with transparent backgrounds, which is the standard format for images without backgrounds.

How accurate is the background removal?

Our AI technology provides highly accurate background removal, especially for images with clear contrast between subject and background. For best results, use images with well-defined edges. You can use the refine options for challenging images like hair, fur, or complex edges.

Can I add a new background after removing the original?

Yes! After removing the background, you can choose to add a new solid color background (white, black) or a gradient background. The transparent PNG can also be used in any graphic design software to add custom backgrounds.

What types of images work best?

The tool works best with images that have good contrast between subject and background. Product photos on plain backgrounds, portraits against solid walls, and clear logos typically yield excellent results.

Does this work on mobile devices?

Yes, the background remover works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface is fully responsive and optimized for all screen sizes.
