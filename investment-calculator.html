Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the investment calculator?

Our calculator uses standard compound interest formulas that are widely accepted in the financial industry. The accuracy depends on the input parameters you provide. While it provides precise mathematical calculations, actual investment returns may vary based on market conditions, fees, and taxes not accounted for in the basic calculation.

What is the difference between lumpsum and SIP calculations?

Lumpsum calculation assumes a one-time investment that grows with compound interest over time. SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) calculation accounts for regular monthly investments where each installment compounds for a different period. SIP benefits from rupee cost averaging and is generally recommended for long-term wealth creation.

How does inflation affect my investment returns?

Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time. Our calculator provides both nominal returns (without inflation) and real returns (inflation-adjusted). The inflation-adjusted value shows what your future investment would be worth in today's purchasing power, which is crucial for retirement planning and long-term goals.

What compounding frequency should I choose?

More frequent compounding (monthly or quarterly) generally yields slightly higher returns than annual compounding. Most mutual funds and investment products compound returns quarterly or monthly. Choose the frequency that matches your actual investment product for accurate calculations.

Is the calculator free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our Investment Calculator is 100% free with no hidden costs or limitations. You can perform unlimited calculations without any registration. We believe in providing valuable financial tools to help everyone make better investment decisions.

Can I save or print my calculation results?

Yes! You can use the "Save Report" button to download a PDF summary of your calculation results. You can also take screenshots of the charts and tables for your records. All calculations are performed locally, so your data remains private and secure.
