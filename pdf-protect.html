About This PDF Protect Tool

Our PDF Protect tool is developed by security experts who understand the importance of document confidentiality. We've created this tool to help businesses, professionals, and individuals secure sensitive PDF documents without compromising on ease of use.

Unlike other online protectors, our tool processes everything locally in your browser using advanced JavaScript libraries. This ensures:

  • Your PDF files never leave your device
  • No file size limits or watermarks added
  • Complete privacy - we cannot access your documents
  • Military-grade 128-bit AES encryption
  • Instant processing without server delays

The tool uses industry-standard encryption algorithms that are compatible with Adobe Acrobat and other PDF readers. Perfect for securing contracts, financial documents, legal papers, personal information, and any sensitive content.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Protect tool completely free?

Yes, absolutely! Our PDF Protect tool is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can protect as many PDF files as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable security tools to everyone.

Are my PDF files safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your documents.

What type of encryption do you use?

We use industry-standard 128-bit AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) encryption, which is the same level of security used by banks and governments. This encryption is compatible with Adobe Acrobat Reader and other PDF viewers.

Can I remove the protection later?

Yes, you can remove the protection if you have the password. Simply use the same tool with the correct password to unlock the PDF. Keep your password safe - without it, the protection cannot be removed, ensuring your document remains secure.

What happens if I forget the password?

Due to the strong encryption used, we cannot recover lost passwords. This is by design to ensure maximum security. We recommend storing your password in a secure password manager or writing it down in a safe place.

Will the protected PDF work on all devices?

Yes, the protected PDF will work on all devices and PDF readers that support standard PDF encryption, including Adobe Acrobat Reader, Preview (Mac), Chrome PDF viewer, and mobile PDF apps. Some basic PDF viewers may prompt for the password but function normally once entered.
