About This PDF Merger & Splitter Tool

Our PDF Merger & Splitter is designed for professionals, students, and anyone who works with PDF documents regularly. We understand the need for quick, reliable document management without compromising security.

Unlike other online PDF tools, our tool processes everything locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your documents never leave your device
  • No file size limits or watermarks
  • Complete privacy and security
  • Instant processing without waiting for uploads

The tool uses advanced PDF processing technology that ensures your documents maintain their original quality, formatting, and security features. Perfect for organizing reports, combining research papers, splitting contracts, or managing any PDF document workflow.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Merger & Splitter really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our PDF Merger & Splitter is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can merge or split as many PDFs as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone manage their documents efficiently.

Are my PDF files secure and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All PDF processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your documents.

How many PDFs can I merge at once?

You can merge up to 10 PDF files simultaneously, with each file up to 50MB in size. For most users, this is more than sufficient for combining multiple documents into a single file. If you need to merge more files, you can process them in batches.

What splitting options are available?

We offer two splitting methods: 1) Split by page range - extract specific pages or ranges (e.g., 1-3, 5, 7-9), and 2) Extract every page - create separate PDF for each page. Both methods preserve the original quality and formatting of your documents.

Will merging or splitting affect PDF quality?

No, the quality of your PDFs remains exactly the same. Our tool preserves all original formatting, images, text quality, and security features. Whether you're merging multiple documents or splitting pages, the output maintains the same quality as the input.

What happens to PDF security features?

Our tool preserves all security features during processing. However, note that if you're splitting a password-protected PDF, you'll need to enter the password first. The resulting files will maintain the same security settings as the original document.
