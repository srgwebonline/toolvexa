Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PDF Merger & Splitter really free?

Yes, our PDF Merger & Splitter is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can merge or split as many PDFs as you need without any restrictions.

Are my PDF files safe and private?

Absolutely! All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security.

What's the maximum file size I can process?

You can process PDF files up to 50MB each. For merging, you can combine up to 20 files at once. Larger files may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Can I reorder PDF pages before merging?

Yes! You can easily reorder PDF files before merging. Use the up and down arrows next to each file in the file list to arrange them in your preferred order.

What splitting options are available?

We offer three splitting methods: 1) By page range (specific pages), 2) By file size (split into parts of certain size), and 3) By page count (equal number of pages per file).
