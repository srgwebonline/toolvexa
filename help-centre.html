Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Toolsedito.

Are Toolsedito tools really free to use?

Yes, all 50+ tools on Toolsedito are completely free to use with no hidden charges, registration requirements, or usage limits. We believe in providing accessible productivity tools to everyone.

How do you protect my privacy when I use your tools?

We use browser-based processing for most tools, which means your files never leave your device. For tools that require server processing, we use secure connections and automatically delete files after processing. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

What file formats are supported by your image tools?

Our image tools support JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, and SVG formats. The Image Converter tool can convert between 15+ different image formats while preserving quality.

Is there a file size limit for PDF processing?

Most PDF tools support files up to 100MB. For optimal performance, we recommend files under 50MB. If you encounter issues with larger files, try our PDF Compressor first to reduce file size.

Do I need to create an account to use Toolsedito?

No, you can use all Toolsedito tools without creating an account. We offer optional account creation for users who want to save their preferences or access additional features, but it's not required.

Can I use Toolsedito on my mobile device?

Yes, Toolsedito is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. All tools are optimized for touch screens and mobile browsers.

How often are new tools added to Toolsedito?

We regularly add new tools based on user feedback and emerging needs. We typically release 2-3 new tools every month. You can follow our blog or subscribe to our newsletter for updates.

What should I do if a tool isn't working properly?

First, try refreshing the page and clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, check our Troubleshooting guide or contact our support team with details about the problem, your browser, and device.
