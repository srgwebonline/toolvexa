About Area Conversion

What is Area?

Area is the amount of space inside the boundary of a flat object or surface. It's measured in square units like square meters (m²), square feet (ft²), acres, and hectares.

How to Use This Tool

Enter a value, select the unit you're converting from, choose the unit you want to convert to, and get instant results. You can also use the quick conversion buttons for common conversions.

Common Uses

Area conversion is essential for real estate, construction, agriculture, land measurement, interior design, and any field that involves measuring surfaces or land areas.
