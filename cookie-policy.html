Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026 | This policy explains how Toolsedito uses cookies and similar technologies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

At Toolsedito, we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and support our marketing efforts.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic site functionality, such as page navigation and access to secure areas. These cookies do not store personal information.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information about page visits and tool usage.

Advertising Cookies

Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track ad campaign performance. These may be set by third-party advertising partners.

Preference Cookies

Remember your preferences (like theme settings) and provide enhanced, more personalized features on subsequent visits.

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use various third-party cookies to report usage statistics, deliver advertisements, and so on. These include:

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. We use the information to compile reports and help us improve the site. The cookies collect information in an anonymous form, including the number of visitors to the site, where visitors have come from, and the pages they visited.

Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements on our site. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites.

Social Media Platforms

We may embed content from social media platforms like Twitter or Facebook, which may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies and you should check the respective privacy policies of these sites for more information.

Your Cookie Choices

You have several options to control or limit how we and our partners use cookies:

  • Browser Controls: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting essential cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
  • Opt-Out Tools: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting YourAdChoices or NAI Consumer Opt-Out.
  • Google Analytics: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" feature that lets you tell websites you do not want to have your online activities tracked. Our site does not currently respond to "Do Not Track" signals.

Cookie Duration

Cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: These are temporary and expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: These remain on your device for a set period specified in the cookie (e.g., 30 days, 1 year, etc.).

We strive to keep cookie durations as short as possible while still providing a good user experience. Essential cookies are typically session-based, while preference cookies may last longer to remember your settings across visits.
