Frequently Asked Questions

Is the plagiarism checker really free?

Yes, our plagiarism checker is completely free to use with no hidden costs or limitations. You can check as much text as you need without any restrictions.

How accurate is the plagiarism detection?

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to compare your text against billions of web pages and documents. While no plagiarism checker is 100% perfect, our tool provides highly accurate results suitable for most purposes including academic papers and professional content.

Is my text stored or shared with others?

No, your privacy is our priority. All text processing happens locally in your browser. Your content is never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

What file formats can I upload?

You can upload text files (.txt), Word documents (.doc, .docx), and PDF files (.pdf). The tool will extract text from these files for plagiarism checking.

What's considered acceptable similarity percentage?

Generally, academic papers should aim for 95%+ originality. Blog posts and articles can have 85%+ originality. Common phrases and quotes will naturally appear as matches, so focus on significant content matches rather than perfect scores.
