Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Plagiarism Checker really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our Plagiarism Checker is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can check as much text as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone create original content.

Is my text safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All text processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your content.

How accurate is the plagiarism check?

Our tool provides a high level of accuracy by checking against web content and using advanced algorithms to detect paraphrased content. While no tool can guarantee 100% accuracy, our results are reliable for identifying potential plagiarism issues that need further investigation.

What's considered a good similarity score?

Generally, a similarity score below 15% is considered excellent, 15-25% is good, 25-40% requires review, and above 40% indicates significant plagiarism. However, context matters - technical papers may naturally have higher similarity due to standard terminology.

Can I check long documents?

Yes! You can check documents up to 10,000 words in one go. For longer documents, we recommend breaking them into sections. The tool handles all common document lengths for academic papers, articles, blog posts, and reports.

Does it check against academic databases?

Our tool primarily checks against publicly available web content. For comprehensive academic checking against published papers and journals, enable the "Include academic databases" option in the settings, though this may slightly increase processing time.
