Frequently Asked Questions

Are Toolvexa tools really free?
Yes! All tools on Toolvexa are completely free to use with no hidden charges or subscription fees. We may display ads to support the platform, but the tools themselves are free.
Do you store or access my uploaded files?
Most Toolvexa tools process files locally in your browser, meaning your files never leave your computer. For tools that require server processing, files are temporarily stored, processed, and automatically deleted. We do not access or view your file contents.
How can I suggest a new tool?
We welcome suggestions for new tools! Please email us at feedback@toolvexa.com with your idea, or use the contact form above and select "Feedback & Suggestions" as the subject.
Can I use Toolvexa for commercial purposes?
Yes, you can use our tools for commercial purposes. However, we don't provide warranties about the accuracy or reliability of the tools. For critical business operations, always verify the results independently.
