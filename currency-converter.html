Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Our exchange rates are sourced from reliable financial data providers and are updated in real-time. While we strive for accuracy, rates can fluctuate rapidly in live markets. For critical transactions, we recommend verifying rates with your bank or financial institution.

Do you charge any fees for currency conversion?

No, our currency converter is completely free to use. We don't charge any fees for conversions. The rates shown are market mid-rates; actual bank rates may include spreads or fees.

How often are exchange rates updated?

Exchange rates are updated in real-time during market hours (Monday-Friday). Rates are refreshed every 5 minutes to ensure accuracy. For currencies with limited trading, rates may be updated less frequently.

Can I convert historical currency rates?

Currently, we only provide current exchange rates. For historical rates, you would need to use specialized financial data services or check with your bank for past conversion rates.

Which currencies are supported?

We support over 160 world currencies including all major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, etc.) and many minor currencies. The dropdown menu shows the most commonly used currencies, but you can search for any currency by its three-letter code.
