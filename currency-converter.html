Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the exchange rates?

Our exchange rates are updated hourly from reliable financial data sources including central banks and major financial institutions. While we strive for accuracy, please note that actual exchange rates may vary slightly depending on your bank or exchange service.

Which currencies do you support?

We support 180+ world currencies including all major currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CNY, INR, AUD, CAD) and many minor currencies. Our database includes currency codes, symbols, and full names for easy identification.

How often are exchange rates updated?

Exchange rates are updated hourly during market hours. For major currency pairs, updates occur more frequently. You can see the last update time displayed below the conversion result.

Do you charge any fees for currency conversion?

No, our currency converter is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or usage limits. The rates shown are mid-market rates without any markup.

Can I use this tool for business transactions?

Yes, our converter is suitable for business planning and estimates. However, for actual transactions, please verify rates with your bank or financial institution as actual rates may include fees and margins.

Is historical data available?

Yes, we provide historical exchange rate data and trends. You can view historical rates for major currency pairs over different time periods (1 week, 1 month, 1 year) to analyze trends and patterns.
