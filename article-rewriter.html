Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Article Rewriter tool work?

Our Article Rewriter uses advanced AI algorithms to understand your text's context and meaning, then rewrites it using different words and sentence structures while preserving the original message. It analyzes synonyms, sentence patterns, and grammar to create unique content.

Is the rewritten content plagiarism-free?

Yes, the tool is designed to create unique content by rephrasing sentences and using different vocabulary. However, we recommend running the output through plagiarism checkers for academic or professional use, as the uniqueness level depends on the complexity of the original text.

What's the maximum text length I can rewrite?

You can rewrite up to 10,000 characters per session. For longer articles, we recommend breaking them into sections and rewriting each separately. This ensures optimal quality and performance.

Does the tool store my text?

No! All processing happens locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or collect any of your text. Your content remains completely private on your device.

Can I use this tool for SEO content?

Absolutely! The tool is excellent for creating SEO-friendly content. Use the "SEO Optimized" preset and enable "Preserve Keywords" to maintain important SEO terms while making the content unique for better search engine rankings.
