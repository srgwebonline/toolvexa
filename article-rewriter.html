Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Article Rewriter completely free?

Yes, absolutely! Our Article Rewriter is 100% free with no hidden costs, usage limits, or watermarks. You can rewrite as much text as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone improve their writing.

Is my text private and secure?

Your privacy is our top priority. All text processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your content.

How does the rewriting algorithm work?

Our algorithm uses a combination of synonym replacement, sentence restructuring, and context-aware paraphrasing. It analyzes your text to understand meaning and context, then generates rewritten versions that maintain the original intent while using different vocabulary and sentence structures.

What's the maximum text length I can rewrite?

You can rewrite up to 10,000 characters at once. For longer texts, simply break them into sections and rewrite each section separately. This ensures optimal processing speed and quality results.

Can I use this for academic papers?

Yes! Use the "Academic" mode for formal papers. However, always review the rewritten text to ensure it meets your specific requirements and maintains academic integrity. The tool is designed to assist with paraphrasing, not to replace proper citation and original work.

Will the rewritten text be plagiarism-free?

Our tool significantly changes the text structure and vocabulary, which helps create unique content. However, for critical applications like academic submissions or published content, we recommend using a plagiarism checker to ensure originality and proper citation of sources.
