Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert with this tool?

+

Our image converter supports all major formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, ICO, and SVG. You can convert between any of these formats seamlessly. The tool handles both lossy and lossless compression formats.

Is there any limit on file size or number of conversions?

+

No, there are no limits! You can convert unlimited images without any restrictions on file size or number of conversions. Our tool handles images up to 50MB efficiently, though we recommend keeping files under 10MB for optimal performance.

How does the image conversion process work?

+

The conversion happens entirely in your browser using HTML5 Canvas API. When you upload an image, it's processed locally without ever leaving your device. This ensures maximum privacy and security while providing instant conversion results.

Will the converted images have watermarks?

+

Absolutely not! All converted images are completely free of watermarks. We believe in providing clean, professional results without any branding or limitations. What you upload is what you get back - just in your desired format.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

+

Yes! Our tool now supports bulk image conversion. You can upload up to 20 images at once and convert them all to your desired format in one go. All converted images can be downloaded as a single ZIP file for convenience.
