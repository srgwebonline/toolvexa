Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Converter really free to use?

Yes, our Image Converter is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can convert as many images as you need without any restrictions.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All image conversion happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support all popular image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, and ICO. You can convert between any of these formats seamlessly.

Does conversion affect image quality?

You can control the quality with our quality slider. For lossless formats (PNG, BMP), quality is preserved. For lossy formats (JPG), you can balance quality and file size.

Can I convert multiple images at once?

Yes! You can upload and convert up to 10 images simultaneously. Our batch processing feature saves you time when working with multiple images.

About Image Converter Tool

What is Toolvexa Image Converter?

Toolvexa Image Converter is a free online tool that helps you convert images between different formats while maintaining quality. It's perfect for web developers, designers, photographers, and anyone who needs to change image formats for different purposes.

Key Features

  • Browser-Based Processing: All conversion happens locally in your browser
  • No File Limits: Convert as many images as you need
  • Multiple Formats: Supports JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, ICO
  • Quality Control: Adjust output quality for lossy formats
  • Batch Processing: Convert multiple images at once
  • Privacy Guaranteed: Your images never leave your device

How It Works

Our tool uses HTML5 Canvas API for image manipulation and format conversion. The process is simple:

  1. Upload your images (drag & drop or browse)
  2. Select target format and quality settings
  3. Click convert and watch the magic happen
  4. Download your converted images

Use Cases

  • Web Development: Convert images to WebP for better performance
  • Social Media: Prepare images in required formats
  • Printing: Convert to high-quality formats for printing
  • Compatibility: Convert images for software that requires specific formats
  • Favicons: Create ICO files for website icons

Technical Information

The tool uses Canvas API for image manipulation and implements format-specific conversion techniques:

  • JPEG encoding with quality control
  • PNG compression with transparency support
  • WebP conversion for modern browsers
  • GIF animation preservation
  • ICO format generation for favicons
