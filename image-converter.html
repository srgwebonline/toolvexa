Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Image Converter tool

General Questions

Is the Image Converter really free?
Yes, our Image Converter is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. You can convert unlimited images without any restrictions.
Are my images safe and private?
Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server.
What image formats do you support?
We support all major image formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, TIFF, SVG, and ICO. You can convert between any of these formats.

Technical Questions

How many images can I convert at once?
You can convert up to 10 images simultaneously using our batch conversion feature. There are no daily limits or restrictions.
Does the converter reduce image quality?
Our converter uses lossless compression whenever possible. For lossy formats like JPG, you can adjust quality settings to balance file size and image quality.
What's the maximum file size?
There's no strict file size limit, but very large images may take longer to process. We recommend images under 50MB for optimal performance.

Usage Questions

Which browsers are supported?
All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. The tool requires JavaScript to be enabled.
Does the tool work offline?
Yes! Once the page is loaded, you can use all conversion features without an internet connection.
Can I convert animated GIFs?
Yes, our tool supports animated GIF conversion. When converting to GIF format, animations are preserved.
