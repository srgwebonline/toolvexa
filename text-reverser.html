Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Text Reverser really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our Text Reverser is 100% free with no hidden costs, limitations, or watermarks. You can reverse as much text as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone with their text manipulation needs.

Is my text safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All text processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device or gets uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your text.

What are the different reversal options?

We offer four main reversal types: 1) Full Text - reverses the entire text character by character, 2) Words Only - reverses the order of words while keeping letters in order, 3) Lines Only - reverses the order of lines, and 4) Letters Only - reverses letters within each word while keeping word order.

What is mirror text?

Mirror text creates text that appears reversed as if seen in a mirror. This is different from simple reversal because it uses special Unicode characters that look like mirrored versions of regular letters. It's perfect for creative social media posts and unique text effects.

Can I reverse very long texts?

Yes, you can reverse texts of virtually any length. Since all processing happens locally in your browser, there are no server-side limits. However, extremely long texts might take a moment to process depending on your device's capabilities.

What practical uses does this tool have?

The Text Reverser has many practical applications: testing palindromes, creating unique text for social media, understanding text encoding, testing software that handles bidirectional text, creating puzzles and games, and analyzing text patterns in linguistics or cryptography.
