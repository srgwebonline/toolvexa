Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Resizer tool really free?

Yes! Our Image Resizer is 100% free to use with no hidden fees, watermarks, or limitations. You can resize as many images as you need without any restrictions.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All image resizing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security for your files.

What image formats are supported?

We support all popular image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. The resized images can be saved in JPG or PNG format based on your quality settings.

Can I maintain aspect ratio while resizing?

Yes! Simply check the "Lock Aspect Ratio" option. When you change either width or height, the other dimension will automatically adjust to maintain the original proportions.

How many images can I resize at once?

You can resize up to 10 images simultaneously using our batch processing feature. This saves time when working with multiple images for websites, social media, or documents.

What are the recommended sizes for social media?

We provide quick presets for popular social media platforms: Instagram (1080x1080), Facebook (1200x628), Twitter (1200x675), LinkedIn (1200x627), and Pinterest (1000x1500).

  • Custom Dimensions: Set exact width and height in pixels

Our tool uses advanced algorithms to resize images while maintaining visual quality. The process is simple and efficient:

  4. Click resize and process all images
  5. Download your perfectly resized images
