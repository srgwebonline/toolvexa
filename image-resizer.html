General Questions

Is the Image Resizer really free?
Yes, our Image Resizer is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or registration required. You can resize unlimited images without any restrictions.
Are my images safe and private?
Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server.
What image formats do you support?
We support all major image formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. You can resize any of these formats with our tool.

Technical Questions

How many images can I resize at once?
You can resize up to 10 images simultaneously using our batch resize feature. There are no daily limits or restrictions.
Does resizing reduce image quality?
Our resizer uses high-quality algorithms to maintain image quality. For lossy formats like JPG, you can adjust quality settings to balance file size and image quality.
Can I maintain aspect ratio?
Yes! You can lock the aspect ratio to maintain proportions. When enabled, changing one dimension automatically adjusts the other.

Usage Questions

Which browsers are supported?
All modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. The tool requires JavaScript to be enabled.
Does the tool work offline?
Yes! Once the page is loaded, you can use all resize features without an internet connection.
What are the common use cases?
Perfect for social media posts, website images, email attachments, profile pictures, thumbnails, and optimizing images for storage.
