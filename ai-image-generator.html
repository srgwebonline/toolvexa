Frequently Asked Questions

Is the AI Image Generator really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our AI Image Generator is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can generate as many images as you want without any restrictions. We believe in democratizing AI technology for everyone.

Can I use the generated images commercially?

Yes, you own the rights to all images you generate with our tool. You can use them for personal projects, commercial purposes, social media, merchandise, or any other application without attribution required.

How long does it take to generate an image?

Generation time varies based on image size and complexity, but typically takes 15-30 seconds. Smaller images (512×512) generate faster than larger ones (1024×1024). The processing happens locally in your browser, so your internet speed doesn't affect generation time.

What kind of prompts work best?

Detailed, descriptive prompts work best. Include information about: subject, style, colors, composition, lighting, mood, and artistic medium. For example: "A majestic lion resting on a cliff at sunset, photorealistic, golden hour lighting, detailed fur, savanna background, epic composition."

Can I generate multiple images at once?

Currently, you can generate one image at a time to ensure quality and proper processing. However, you can generate multiple images sequentially and they will all be saved in your session until you clear them. Use the "Download All" button to save all generated images at once.

Are there any content restrictions?

Our AI is designed to create safe, appropriate content. Please use the tool responsibly and avoid generating harmful, offensive, or inappropriate content. The tool may refuse to generate images for certain restricted prompts to maintain community standards.
