Frequently Asked Questions

What is an XML sitemap and why do I need one?

An XML sitemap is a file that lists all the important pages of your website, helping search engines like Google discover and crawl them more efficiently. It's essential for SEO because it ensures search engines can find all your content, especially pages that might not be easily discoverable through internal links.

Is the sitemap generator really free to use?

Yes, our Sitemap Generator is 100% free with no hidden costs, limitations, or watermarks. You can generate as many sitemaps as you need without any restrictions.

Do you store or access my website data?

No, all processing happens locally in your browser. We don't store your website URLs, crawl your site from our servers, or access any of your data. Your privacy and security are guaranteed.

What file formats are supported for download?

We generate standard XML sitemap files (.xml) that are compatible with all search engines including Google, Bing, and Yahoo. The generated file follows the official sitemap protocol specification.

How do I submit my sitemap to Google?

After downloading your sitemap, upload it to your website's root directory (e.g., https://yourwebsite.com/sitemap.xml). Then submit it to Google Search Console under the "Sitemaps" section. We'll provide detailed instructions with your download.
