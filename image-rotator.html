Is the Image Rotator tool really free?

Yes, our Image Rotator is completely free with no hidden charges. You can rotate, flip, and mirror as many images as you want without any limitations or watermarks.

No, our tool uses lossless rotation algorithms that preserve the original quality of your image. The rotated image will maintain the same resolution and quality as the original.
