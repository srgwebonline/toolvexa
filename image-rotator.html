About This Image Rotator Tool

Our Image Rotator is built by web design experts who understand the importance of properly oriented images. We've developed this tool specifically to help photographers, designers, and content creators adjust their images without needing complex software.

Unlike other online rotators, our tool processes everything locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your images never leave your device
  • No file size limits or watermarks
  • Complete privacy and security
  • Instant processing without waiting for uploads

The tool uses advanced canvas rendering that preserves image quality while applying rotations and flips. Perfect for fixing photos taken in the wrong orientation, creating mirrored effects, or preparing images for presentations and social media.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Rotator really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our Image Rotator is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can rotate as many images as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone improve their digital content.

Are my images safe and private?

Your privacy is our top priority. All image processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security - we don't store, share, or access any of your files.

What image formats do you support?

We support all popular image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. The rotated images are saved in their original format to preserve quality.

Will rotation affect image quality?

Our rotation algorithm uses high-quality canvas rendering that preserves image quality. Unlike some tools that degrade quality with each rotation, our tool maintains the original image quality throughout the process.

Can I rotate multiple images at once?

This tool is designed for single image rotation to ensure the best user experience and performance. For batch processing, we recommend rotating images one at a time or using our Image Editor tool for more complex workflows.

What's the maximum image size I can rotate?

You can rotate images up to 10MB in size. For best performance with very large images, we recommend using the tool on a desktop or laptop computer with sufficient memory.
