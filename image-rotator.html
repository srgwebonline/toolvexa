Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Rotator really free to use?

Yes, our Image Rotator is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can rotate as many images as you need without any restrictions.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All image processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. This ensures complete privacy and security.

What image formats do you support?

We support all popular image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. The rotated images maintain their original format quality.

No, our rotation algorithm preserves image quality. We use high-quality interpolation methods to ensure your images look perfect after rotation.

Can I rotate multiple images at once?

Currently, we support single image rotation for precise control. For multiple images, you can process them one by one quickly using our efficient interface.

About Image Rotator Tool

What is Toolvexa Image Rotator?

Toolvexa Image Rotator is a free online tool that helps you rotate, flip, and mirror images with precision. It's perfect for photographers, designers, and anyone who needs to adjust image orientation for various purposes.

Key Features

  • Browser-Based Processing: All rotation happens locally in your browser
  • Multiple Rotation Options: Rotate by any angle, flip horizontally/vertically
  • Format Support: Supports JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, and more
  • Quality Preservation: Maintains original image quality
  • Instant Preview: See changes in real-time
  • Privacy Guaranteed: Your images never leave your device

How It Works

Our tool uses advanced HTML5 Canvas API to rotate images while preserving quality. The process is simple:

  1. Upload your image (drag & drop or browse)
  2. Choose rotation angle or flip option
  3. Preview the result instantly
  4. Download your rotated image

Use Cases

  • Photo Correction: Fix incorrectly oriented photos
  • Design Projects: Create mirrored effects for designs
  • Social Media: Prepare images for different platforms
  • Documentation: Rotate scanned documents
  • Creative Projects: Create artistic mirror effects

Technical Information

The tool uses Canvas API for image manipulation and implements advanced rotation algorithms including:

  • High-quality interpolation for smooth rotation
  • Matrix transformation for precise control
  • Lossless rotation for quality preservation
  • Real-time preview with zoom controls
