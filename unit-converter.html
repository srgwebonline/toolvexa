Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the unit converter?

Our unit converter uses precise conversion factors and formulas to ensure maximum accuracy. For temperature conversions, we use exact formulas (not approximations) to guarantee correct results.

Can I convert between different categories?

No, you can only convert within the same category (e.g., meters to feet within Length). For cross-category conversions (like volume to weight), you would need substance density information which is not supported by basic unit conversion.

Does the converter support metric and imperial systems?

Yes! Our converter supports both metric and imperial units across all categories. You can easily convert between systems, such as kilometers to miles, kilograms to pounds, or liters to gallons.

How many decimal places are shown in results?

Results are shown with up to 6 decimal places for precision. However, for very large or very small numbers, scientific notation may be used to maintain readability.

Can I use the converter on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our unit converter is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for optimal usability.
