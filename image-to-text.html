About This OCR Image to Text Tool

Our OCR (Optical Character Recognition) tool is built using advanced Tesseract.js technology to provide accurate text extraction from images. We've developed this tool specifically to help students, researchers, professionals, and anyone who needs to convert images of text into editable formats.

Unlike other online OCR tools, our tool processes everything locally in your browser. This means:

  • Your images never leave your device - complete privacy
  • No file size limits or watermarks added
  • Instant processing without waiting for uploads
  • Support for multiple languages and character sets

The tool uses state-of-the-art OCR algorithms that can recognize text from screenshots, scanned documents, photographs, and even handwritten text with reasonable accuracy. Perfect for digitizing documents, extracting text from screenshots, converting printed materials to digital format, and much more.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the OCR tool really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! Our OCR Image to Text tool is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can extract text from as many images as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable tools to help everyone with their digital work.

What types of images work best with OCR?

For best results, use clear images with good contrast between text and background. Horizontal text works better than angled text. High-resolution images (300 DPI or higher) typically yield better accuracy. The tool works well with screenshots, scanned documents, photographs of documents, and printed materials.

What languages does the OCR support?

Our tool supports multiple languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, and Chinese Simplified. You can select the appropriate language from the settings before extracting text for better accuracy. The default language is English.

How accurate is the text extraction?

Accuracy depends on image quality, text clarity, font type, and language. With clear images and standard fonts, accuracy can reach 95% or higher. For handwritten text or low-quality images, accuracy may be lower. Use the pre-processing options to enhance your images for better results.

Can I extract text from handwritten documents?

Yes, our OCR tool can recognize clear, legible handwriting, though accuracy may vary compared to printed text. For best results with handwriting, ensure the writing is neat, uses dark ink on light background, and the image is well-lit. Complex cursive handwriting may have lower recognition rates.

Is there a file size limit for images?

We recommend images under 10MB for optimal performance. Larger images may take longer to process. The tool supports common image formats including JPG, PNG, BMP, and WebP. All processing happens in your browser, so there are no server-side restrictions.
