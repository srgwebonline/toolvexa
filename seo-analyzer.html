Frequently Asked Questions

How does the SEO Analyzer work?

Our SEO Analyzer simulates a search engine crawler to analyze your website's structure, meta tags, content, and technical aspects. It checks over 50 different SEO factors to provide a comprehensive report.

Is my website data safe?

Yes! We don't store any of your website data. All analysis happens in real-time and results are displayed only in your browser. No data is sent to our servers.

How accurate is the analysis?

Our analysis is based on Google's SEO guidelines and industry best practices. While it provides accurate technical analysis, actual search rankings depend on many factors including competition and content quality.

Can I analyze password-protected websites?

No, the tool can only analyze publicly accessible websites. It cannot bypass authentication or access restricted areas of a website.

How often should I analyze my website?

We recommend analyzing your website at least once a month, or whenever you make significant changes to your site structure, content, or meta tags.
