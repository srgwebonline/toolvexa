Frequently Asked Questions

Which tax regime should I choose for AY 2024-25?

The new tax regime is now the default option from AY 2024-25. However, you can still choose the old regime if you have significant deductions. Our calculator compares both regimes and recommends the optimal choice based on your income and deductions.

What are the tax slabs under the new regime?

For AY 2024-25, the new regime tax slabs are: 0% up to ₹3L, 5% for ₹3-7L, 10% for ₹7-10L, 15% for ₹10-12L, 20% for ₹12-15L, and 30% above ₹15L. There's a standard deduction of ₹50,000 available under both regimes.

How is HRA exemption calculated?

HRA exemption is the minimum of: 1) Actual HRA received, 2) 50% of basic salary (metro) or 40% (non-metro), 3) Rent paid minus 10% of basic salary. Use our HRA calculator for accurate calculation.

What deductions are available in the old regime?

Major deductions under old regime include: Section 80C (₹1.5L), 80D (health insurance), HRA, home loan interest (80EEA), NPS (80CCD), education loan interest (80E), and standard deduction of ₹50,000.
