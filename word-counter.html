Frequently Asked Questions

How does the word counter work?

Our word counter analyzes text in real-time using JavaScript. It splits text into words based on spaces and punctuation, counts characters (with and without spaces), identifies sentences and paragraphs, and calculates reading/speaking times based on average speeds.

What is considered a "word" in the count?

A word is defined as any sequence of characters separated by spaces or punctuation. Numbers can be included or excluded based on your settings. Common words (like "the", "and", "a") can also be filtered out for more meaningful analysis.

How is reading time calculated?

Reading time is calculated using the average reading speed of 200 words per minute. This is a standard metric used by most reading platforms. Speaking time uses 130 words per minute, which is the average speaking rate for presentations.

Is my text stored or saved anywhere?

No! All text analysis happens locally in your browser. Your text is never sent to any server or stored anywhere. This ensures complete privacy and security for your content.

What is word frequency analysis?

Word frequency analysis shows you how often each word appears in your text. This helps identify overused words, check keyword density for SEO, and improve writing variety. The analysis can be case-sensitive or case-insensitive based on your settings.
