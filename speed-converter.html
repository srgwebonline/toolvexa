Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the speed converter?

Our speed converter uses precise conversion factors with up to 10 decimal places for maximum accuracy. All conversions follow international standards and are suitable for scientific, engineering, and everyday use.

What speed units are supported?

We support all major speed units including kilometers per hour (km/h), miles per hour (mph), meters per second (m/s), knots (kn), feet per second (ft/s), Mach number, and even the speed of light. Additional specialized units can be added upon request.

How do you handle Mach number conversions?

Mach number conversions are based on the speed of sound at sea level (approximately 340.29 m/s or 1225 km/h). For precise scientific calculations, we recommend specifying the exact conditions as Mach varies with temperature and altitude.

Can I convert very large or small speed values?

Yes! Our converter handles values from 0.000000001 to 1,000,000,000,000. This covers everything from snail speeds to astronomical velocities. The tool automatically adjusts decimal places for optimal readability.

Is my data stored when I use the converter?

No! All calculations happen locally in your browser. We don't store, transmit, or collect any of your input data. Your privacy is completely protected and no information leaves your device.
