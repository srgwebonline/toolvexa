Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the search volume data?

Our search volume data is based on aggregated data from multiple sources and provides reliable estimates for keyword popularity. While no tool can provide 100% accurate search volume, our data is calibrated to give you a realistic understanding of keyword demand.

What does "competition" mean in the results?

Competition score indicates how difficult it would be to rank for that keyword. Low competition means fewer websites are targeting that keyword, making it easier to rank. High competition indicates many established websites are competing for that keyword.

Can I export the keyword data?

Yes! You can export your keyword research results in multiple formats including CSV, JSON, and PDF. The export feature includes all keyword data, metrics, and analysis.

How many keywords can I research at once?

You can research up to 100 keywords in a single analysis session. For batch processing of larger keyword lists, consider using our API or professional plan features.

Is this tool really free to use?

Yes, our Keyword Research Tool is 100% free with no hidden costs or limitations. You can use all features without registration or payment.

Toolvexa Keyword Research Tool is a comprehensive solution for discovering profitable keywords for SEO, content marketing, and PPC campaigns. Our tool helps you identify high-potential keywords with the right balance of search volume and competition.

Key Features

  • Search Volume Data: Get estimated monthly search volumes for keywords
  • Competition Analysis: Understand keyword difficulty and competition levels
  • Cost-Per-Click (CPC) Data: View estimated advertising costs for keywords
  • Trend Analysis: Track keyword popularity over time
  • Keyword Suggestions: Discover related keywords and long-tail variations
  • Multi-Country Support: Research keywords for different geographic markets
  • Export Functionality: Download results in multiple formats

How It Works

  1. Enter a seed keyword or phrase

  3. Click "Analyze Keywords" to generate results
  4. Explore keyword data, metrics, and suggestions
  5. Export your research for further analysis
