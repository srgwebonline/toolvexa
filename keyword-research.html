Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Keyword Research Tool really free?

Yes, absolutely! Our Keyword Research Tool is 100% free with no hidden costs, usage limits, or registration requirements. You can research as many keywords as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing valuable SEO tools to help businesses and creators succeed online.

How accurate is the search volume data?

Our search volume estimates are based on multiple data sources and algorithms to provide accurate relative volume indicators. While no tool can provide exact Google search volumes (only Google has that data), our estimates are reliable for comparative analysis and identifying high-opportunity keywords.

What does the competition score mean?

The competition score (0-100%) represents how difficult it is to rank for a keyword. Lower scores (0-30%) indicate low competition keywords that are easier to rank for. Medium scores (31-70%) represent moderate competition, and high scores (71-100%) indicate highly competitive keywords that are difficult to rank for without strong domain authority.

Can I export the keyword data?

Yes! You can export your keyword research results in CSV or Excel format with one click. The exported files include all keyword metrics (keyword, search volume, CPC, competition, intent) for easy integration with your SEO tools and content planning workflows.

What are long-tail keywords?

Long-tail keywords are longer, more specific keyword phrases (usually 3+ words) that have lower search volume but higher conversion rates. They're less competitive and easier to rank for, making them excellent for targeted content and niche marketing. Examples: "best running shoes for flat feet women" or "how to grow organic tomatoes in containers".

How often is the keyword data updated?

Our keyword database is updated monthly to reflect changes in search trends and volumes. However, keep in mind that search behavior can change rapidly, especially for trending topics. For the most current data, we recommend researching keywords regularly as part of your ongoing SEO strategy.
