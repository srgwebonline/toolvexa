Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Image Cropper completely free?

Yes, our Image Cropper is 100% free with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. You can crop as many images as you need without any restrictions. We believe in providing professional tools to everyone for free.

How does the browser-based cropping work?

All cropping operations happen locally in your browser using HTML5 Canvas technology. Your images are never uploaded to any server - they stay entirely on your device. This ensures maximum privacy and security while providing instant results.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. The cropped image can be downloaded as a high-quality JPG or PNG file, depending on your needs.

Can I crop images to specific dimensions?

Yes! You can enter exact pixel dimensions for width and height. You can also choose from preset aspect ratios for social media (Instagram, Facebook), printing standards, or mobile devices. The aspect ratio lock feature maintains proportions when resizing.

Will cropping reduce image quality?

Our cropping algorithm maintains the original image quality. Since we're using HTML5 Canvas with high-quality rendering, there's no quality loss during the cropping process. The downloaded image will be as sharp as your original.

Can I undo or redo cropping actions?

Yes, you can reset the crop selection at any time using the "Reset" button. You can also adjust the selection area by dragging it or using the resize handles. For multiple undo/redo steps, you can simply readjust the selection as needed.
