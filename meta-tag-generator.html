Frequently Asked Questions

Why are meta tags important for SEO?

Meta tags help search engines understand your page content, which can improve your search rankings. Title tags and meta descriptions are displayed in search results and influence click-through rates. Open Graph and Twitter Cards control how your content appears when shared on social media.

What's the ideal length for meta tags?

Title Tag: 50-60 characters (Google typically displays 50-60 characters)
Meta Description: 120-160 characters (Google may truncate after 160)
Open Graph Title: 60 characters optimal
Open Graph Description: 200 characters optimal

Do I need both Open Graph and Twitter Cards?

Yes, it's recommended to include both. Facebook, LinkedIn, and other platforms use Open Graph tags, while Twitter uses Twitter Card tags. Including both ensures your content displays optimally across all social media platforms.

Are meta keywords still important for SEO?

Major search engines like Google no longer use meta keywords for ranking. However, some smaller search engines might still consider them. It's generally safe to include them, but focus your efforts on title tags, meta descriptions, and quality content instead.

How do I add these meta tags to my website?

Copy the generated code from this tool and paste it into the <head> section of your HTML document, typically between the opening <head> and closing </head> tags. If you're using a CMS like WordPress, you can use SEO plugins like Yoast SEO or Rank Math that provide fields for these meta tags.
